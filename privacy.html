Privacy Policy

Cave Game Request Data Fetcher

Effective Date: 05/28/24

1. Information We Collect

The Extension collects the following types of information:

  • Request Data: The Extension intercepts and processes specific network requests made to certain endpoints on the `cave.wolf.game` domain. The data collected includes details such as caveId, id, history, position, coordinates, energy, visible data, totalSteps, and diggable status.

2. How We Use Your Information

The collected information is used to:

  • WebSocket Communication: Send processed data to a WebSocket server (`wss://cavegame.slkzgm.com`) for further processing and analysis. This helps in enhancing your gaming experience by providing detailed insights and data relevant to the Cave game.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

The Extension does not share or disclose your information with third parties, except as necessary to:

  • WebSocket Server: Transmit data to the specified WebSocket server (`wss://cavegame.slkzgm.com`) for the intended purpose of the Extension.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is completely secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
